Duraseal Block Jointing Mortar

Back

A thin layer jointing mortar perfectly binds block with a thin layer of 3 mm to 5 mm as against 18 mm (approx) of conventional mortar. The product is specially designed to provide higher bonding as a result of higher tensile strength. It is premixed, easy to apply, high quality mortar comprising of cement, graded sand and admixtures.

Features
  • Uniform and consistent quality
  • Binds perfectly
  • Thin layer
  • No shrinkage cracks
  • Watertight joints
Benefits
  • No curing required
  • Fast and economical, substantial saving as compared to conventional mortar
  • Ready for subsequent application in 24 hours
